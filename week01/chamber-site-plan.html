Albanian Chamber of Commerce.

This name represents the country in which the chamber operates.

The primary aim of the Albanian Chamber of Commerce is to promote and support the local business community. This can include a wide range of activities such as networking, advocacy, promote and organize events in order to create a supportive environment for local bussinesses to thrive. It will also serve as source of information for local businesses in order to provide networking opportunities, training and development programs, and other resources to help businesses succeed.
